Our Story

It all started in December 2017, at a movie theater. We were 15 and 14, complete strangers, and clearly too young to know what we were doing. Sarah was busy stealing my hat and arguing with me like it was a full-time job. I, of course, was just a charming baseball boy, completely unaware that this would turn into something that lasted way longer than I ever expected.

We can’t really remember the exact moment we fell in love, mostly because it didn’t feel like a “moment.” It was more like a series of increasingly questionable decisions that somehow worked out. We became inseparable, spending every waking second together, driving our friends insane and confusing anyone who tried to understand us.

Our adventures? Oh, where to start: sneaking into her house every morning in high school to escort her to school, dressing up as each other (and yes, it looked ridiculous), buying our first house together, celebrating our first Halloween there, and taking a trip to Europe.

What I love most about Sarah is that she is unapologetically herself. She doesn’t care what anyone thinks, and somehow I get to be along for the ride. What Sarah loves about me is that I can always make her laugh.

That is our story so far: messy, ridiculous, and entirely ours. Now we are ready for the next chapter, wedding day included, with all the love, laughter, and absurdity that got us here.
